## Favorite Existing Apps - Categorize and Evaluate
### Instagram
   - **Category:** Photo & Video / Social 
   - **Mobile:** Website is view only, uses camera, mobile first experience.
   - **Story:** Allows users to share their lives in pictures and enhance their content with filters
   - **Market:** Anyone that takes pictures could enjoy this app. Ability to follow and hashtag based on interests and categories allows users with unique interests to engage with relevant content.
   - **Habit:** Users can post throughout the day many times. Features like "Stories" encourage more candid posting as well. Users can explore endless pictures in any category imaginable whenever they want. Very habbit forming!
   - **Scope:** Instagram started out extremely narrow focused, just posting pics and viewing feeds. Has expanded to a somewhat larger scope including "Instagram Stories" (a la SnapChat) and messenger features.

## New App Ideas - Evaluate and Categorize
1. Fitness Application
   - **Description**: An application that keeps track of fitness goals and actual in-gym exercises. It allows for logging of sets and reps in exercises to monitor your activity. May feature a meal-planner generator.
   - **Category:** Health / Fitness
   - **Mobile:** Mobile is necessary for the easy access to logging of users health journey and exercises. Users use the app to monitor their sets/reps and health journey.
   - **Story:** Helps users stay on top of their exercise progress when working out and allowing for meal planning based on their input information enhancing their fitness journey. 
   - **Market:** Any gym-going individual upto gym coaches helping keep track of student progress. 
   - **Habit:** Gym-goers are using this daily or whenever they are at the gym working out as well as when they need recommendations for meals based on the information they input about themselves.
   - **Scope:** V1, one version, is all that is needed as it is for the user themselves and they control what they want to log or what not to log. It is flexible to their choice.

2. Creativity Art App
   - **Description**: An application that has daily challenges and prompts for you to draw, or create to then share your creation onto the app.
   - **Category:** Social / Creativity
   - **Mobile:** Mobile is necessary for the easy access to art challenge prompts for the user and easy access to posting.
   - **Story:** Allows any users the ability to use prompts to guide their creativity and post it so others can view it and they can view others.
   - **Market:** Any artist who enjoys daily challenge prompts to guide them on what to create.
   - **Habit:** Can be used daily for daily art prompts to enchance creativity.
   - **Scope:** V1, one version, is all that is needed as it is for the user themselves to post and receive challenge prompts and then a database is used to display posts to other users.
3. Learning Application
   - **Description**: An application that lets you choose from various topics and gives short 1-5 minute lessons on said topics to expand your knowledge. Allows for quizzing after said lessons are done. Has correct answer streaks and more
   - **Category:** Education 
   - **Mobile:** Mobile is necessary for the easy access to the quick lessons on various topics for the user
   - **Story:** Allows any users the ability to gain quick knowledge and test themselves on it shortly after as a great way for quick learning.
   - **Market:** Any individual who is always curious to learn, great alternative for social media as it is a quick-learn platform.
   - **Habit:** Users can do it as many times as they want, there is no limit, but daily streaks keep track of learning progress.
   - **Scope:** V1, one version, is all that is needed as it is for the user themselves to pick their preferred topic and learn various things about it.
